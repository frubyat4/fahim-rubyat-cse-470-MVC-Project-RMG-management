Production

Production process is the most important process in this company. Every worker is trained and know their jobs very well. We have training programs for our workers every 6 months and our production team evaluates the workers performance on weekly basis. We take every measures to make sure our product is made to perfection by the production department.

Production Manager
FBN Industries & ltd

Quality Checking

Every single product of ours is checked very thoroughly. If the product quality does not match with our quality level, the product is send back to production process again in order to create the level of quality product that our company requires. We have very capable workers to check every single product that comes out of production and check the product intensively. Same product is checked 3 times by different workers to ensure the quality of the product our production team has made.

Quality Manager
FBN Industries & ltd

Shipment

Our shipment department handles orders very carefully so that the product does not get damaged by any means. Our workers in shipment department has gone through several training process in order to learn about handling product and different scenarios they can face during the shipment process. They are well trained and will make sure that the product gets shipped properly.

Shipment Manager
FBN Industries & ltd
